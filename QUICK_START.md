# 🚀 Quick Start: Share on WhatsApp Status (3 Steps!)

## ⚡ Fastest Way (5 Minutes Total)

---

## STEP 1: Get Your URL (2 minutes)

1. Open: **https://app.netlify.com/drop**
2. Drag your `Landing-pages` folder onto the page
3. Wait for upload
4. **COPY THE URL** (e.g., `https://abc123.netlify.app`)

---

## STEP 2: Share on WhatsApp (1 minute)

1. Open **WhatsApp** on your phone
2. Tap **"Status"** tab (bottom)
3. Tap **"+"** button
4. Tap **"Link"** icon (🔗) or select "Link" option
5. **Paste your URL**
6. Add caption:
   ```
   💧 Premium Water Purifiers
   Tap to view all products! 👇
   ```
7. Tap **"Send"** ✓

---

## STEP 3: Done! ✅

When people see your status:
- They see a **clickable preview card**
- They **tap it**
- Your **full landing page opens**
- They can **click WhatsApp buttons** to chat!
